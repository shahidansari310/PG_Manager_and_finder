Who We Are

PGFinder is a web-based Paying Guest (PG) search platform developed to help students and bachelors find safe, affordable, and comfortable accommodations in new cities. The platform was created to solve the common problems faced during manual PG searching, such as unreliable information, broker dependency, and time-consuming visits.

PGFinder provides a centralized interface where users can explore PG listings, compare rent, view facilities, and access location and contact details easily. The system is designed with simplicity and usability in mind, making it ideal for first-time users and students relocating for education or work.

This project is developed as a front-end prototype using HTML and CSS, demonstrating the structure and workflow of an online PG finder system. It serves as a foundation for future enhancements such as database integration, authentication, and online booking features.

Our Mission

To simplify the PG accommodation search process by offering a transparent, student-focused, and digital-first platform that reduces effort, time, and dependency on brokers.
